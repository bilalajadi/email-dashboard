Deliverability Metrics
These metrics determine if AWS will suspend your sending. Keep bounce and complaint rates low to maintain good sender reputation.
Bounce Rate --
--%
% of emails that couldn't be delivered (invalid addresses, full inboxes). High bounce = sending to bad addresses.
Target: <2% | Warning: 5% | AWS Suspends: 10%
Complaint Rate --
--%
% of recipients who clicked "Report as Spam". This is the worst signal - means people don't want your emails.
Target: <0.05% | Warning: 0.08% | AWS Suspends: 0.1%
Open Rate --
--%
% of recipients who opened your email. Measures engagement and subject line effectiveness.
Good: >25% | Average: 15-25% | Low: <15%
Click Rate --
--%
% of recipients who clicked a link in your email. Shows how compelling your content is.
Good: >3% | Average: 1-3% | Low: <1%
DNS Authentication
These DNS records prove your emails are legitimate and not forged. If any fail, your emails may go to spam or be rejected.
SPF
--
Tells email providers which servers can send from niyyah.app (like a guest list)
DKIM
--
Digital signature proving the email wasn't tampered with (like a wax seal)
DMARC
--
Policy for what to do if SPF/DKIM fail (reject, quarantine, or allow)
